❝ Illness can change life, but it doesn't have to define it ❞

Facing a physical illness is a major life challenge. Whether sudden or expected, illness brings practical, emotional, and social changes. This page offers steps to navigate diagnosis, treatment, recovery, and adjusting day-to-day life with compassion and realistic plans.

Practical steps and emotional support to help you move forward

PHASE 1: Diagnosis & Early Care

  • Clarify diagnosis: Ask questions, request summaries, and get a written care plan.
  • Build your team: Identify primary clinicians, specialists, and support contacts.
  • Handle logistics: Organize meds, appointments, transport, and insurance details.
Practical organisation reduces emotional load.

PHASE 2: Treatment & Recovery

  • Follow treatment: Attend therapy/appointments and follow medication plans.
  • Pacing: Set realistic activity goals and allow gradual progression.
  • Mental support: Consider counseling, peer groups, or support lines.
Recovery balances medical care with self-compassion.

PHASE 3: Rehabilitation & Adaptation

  • Rehab planning: Work with therapists to set measurable goals.
  • Home adjustments: Arrange practical supports and helpful devices.
  • Work & benefits: Explore accommodations and any eligible supports.
Focus on function and quality of life.
