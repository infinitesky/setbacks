Bankruptcy

Phased guidance: consider options carefully, manage the process, then rebuild credit and finances.

PHASE 1: Consider & Prepare

  • Explore alternatives: negotiate, debt management, or settlement before filing.
  • Get informed: understand chapter differences and eligibility in your jurisdiction.
  • Document finances: collect income, assets, and liability records for advisors and courts.

PHASE 2: File & Navigate

  • Legal counsel: consult a reputable attorney or counselor for filings and paperwork.
  • Follow process: attend required hearings and complete trustee requirements.
  • Protect essentials: understand exemptions and which assets you may retain.
Accurate filings reduce delays and complications.

PHASE 3: Rebuild

  • Credit repair: check reports, dispute errors, and consider secured credit options.
  • Budget reset: rebuild savings and set conservative spending targets.
  • Learn lessons: adopt financial habits that prevent recurrence.
Rebuilding takes time; consistency matters more than speed.
