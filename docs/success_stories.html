Failure is not the alternative to success. It’s something to be avoided, but it’s also only a temporary setback on a bigger, more significant course. Everybody encounters failure at one point or another. What truly matters is how you react to and learn from that failure.

At SETBACKS.ORG, part of our goal is to share platform to discuss issues and state of mind which is stressed, anxious, and depressed from setbacks in life. In addition to connecting with each other, we want to share stories of hope and inspiration, as well as provide content that shows you’re not alone, and it’s okay to reach out for help, share and motivate others.

To that end, we want to share your stories, and how you’ve managed to overcome your own battle and setbacks. When you approach the sharing of your thoughts and feelings with others, please be with an attitude of respect for yourself and for the other person. This will create a healthy discussion atmosphere.

  • It can be a personal story
  • It can be about a wonderful organization that helped
  • It can be about what schools and colleges are doing in your area
  • It can be things that make you happy, and may help others when they’re down

If you’d like to learn more, or have an idea you’d like to share on how we can help those who may be afraid to reach out, simply use the form to the right and we’ll be back in touch with you soon.

Thank you.
